About Side Scroller Sprint

Side Scroller Sprint is a fast-paced HTML5 endless runner game. Test your reflexes by jumping over obstacles in a neon-styled world. Compete for the high score on our global leaderboard powered by Firebase. Master the jump mechanics to survive longer and speed up as you go!
